Apply for Sreeleathers franchise

The history of Sreeleathers dates back almost a century. It was a time when India was striving to fight the British rule in India. Late Suresh Chandra Dey, the father of Sreeleathers, was a freedom fighter and his only aim was to help India gain complete independence.

Sreeleathers is one of the leading shoe and leather accessories brands in India. The company is also looking to expand its network with like-minded partners.It is a Kolkata-based company. Today, Sreeleathers caters to public demand not only with the widest range of shoes but also with allied products and accessories.

Product & Services

The company manufactures, distributes and sells a wide range of leather items including shoes, sandals, bags, belts, purse, wallet, etc.

Sreeleathers Services

Area Required

  • The area required to open a Sreeleathers Store is 1500 – 4000 Sq.ft which should include a franchise store and a counter.
  • The built-up area requirement differs depending upon the location of your Franchise franchise store.
  • Minimum staff required to work at a Sreeleathers franchise store is 10 to 12 people operate your franchise store.
  • Help center access

Skills Required

  • Individuals who are passionate and have a desire to grow and be a part of the fast Service growing industry.
  • The people should be willing to accept the challenges faced on a daily basis and should be able to work well with other people.
  • The employees should have good communication skills, culinary cooking skills and should be courteous to the customers.
  • The candidate should at least be 10th pass.

Training Required

  • Sreeleathers provides 1 week classroom and on-the-job training to all its Franchise employees.
  • The training includes soft skill training, customer handling, improving their culinary skills and how to achieve their sale target effectively and efficiently.
  • Phone and email support
